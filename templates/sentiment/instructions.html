Instructions

You can explore the relationships between the variables you specified for your model by clicking on the above options. To get an overview of all the signals, press Overview. To see correlations of your signals across a time series, press Correlation Graphs. To see the correlation matrix for your model, press Correlation Tables. For all VAR related calculations see: the Lag Order Tables, which shows the lag order selection calculation; the Stationary section, which shows the results of the variable stationary tests; VAR Coefficients, which shows the results of the VAR analysis; and Granger Causality, where you can determine the causal relationships between the signals.
